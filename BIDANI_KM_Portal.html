✅ Success
Leyte barangay reaches zero acute malnutrition for the first time in 6 years
After sustained PNEA implementation in Barangay San Jose, we are proud to report that our latest nutrition monitoring shows zero acute malnutrition cases. This was made possible through the lifecycle approach, consistent BHW training, and community engagement facilitated by BIDANI. Happy to share our documentation with fellow SUCs.

Why This Platform Exists
Bridging the Knowledge Gaps in the BIDANI Network
Based on our network knowledge audit, this platform was specifically designed to address the key KM challenges identified across partner SUCs.
🔗
No SUC-to-SUC Communication
The Community Board creates direct peer channels between all 11 SUCs — not just UPLB to SUC flows.

📂
Materials Hard to Find
